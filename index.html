How PulseAI works

From raw customer and market data to actionable recommendations and continuous validation.

01

Data & ETL

Customer data, social signals, competitor data, and structured processing.

02

AI Analysis

NLP sentiment, ML prediction, and customer segmentation.

03

Behaviour Prediction

Purchase intent, demand forecasts, price sensitivity, and target audiences.

04

Recommendations

Product improvement, pricing, marketing strategy, and launch timing.

05

Validation Loop

Monitor KPIs, check deviations, tune models, and learn continuously.

Three AI use cases

PulseAI turns customer and market signals into decisions teams can act on before problems become expensive.

01 · Predicting User Behavior

Anticipate churn, upgrades, disengagement, and next actions.

  • Churn-risk scores
  • Next-best-action recommendations
  • Behavioural segmentation

02 · Validating Initiatives

Stress-test features, pricing, and campaigns before committing full resources.

  • Adoption and sentiment forecasts
  • Risk flags by segment
  • Go/no-go decision support

03 · Satisfaction + Revenue

Identify which pricing, service, and product levers can improve both CX and commercial outcomes.

  • Ranked satisfaction drivers
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Revenue-impact prioritization
